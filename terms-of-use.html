Strategy Engagement

A 360° deep dive into your food business — uncovering challenges, identifying opportunities, and creating a clear path forward.

What’s Included:

  • Business Health Analysis: Deep assessment of your P&L, pricing, cost structure, team productivity, and margins
  • Focused Problem Solving: Solve critical issues like supply chain bottlenecks, team turnover, or cash burn
  • Business Reinvention: Rethink your model, rework your product-market fit, or pivot as needed
  • Profit Viability: Identify and improve revenue levers, reduce leakage
  • Growth Viability: Evaluate scalability across locations, channels, or formats
  • New Business Idea Access: Explore and stress-test expansion concepts

Format: Virtual or On-Site

Best For: Businesses facing stagnation, losses, or planning expansion

Duration: Hourly / Weekly engagements for at least 2-3 months.

Results You Can Expect:

  • Clear visibility into your business strengths and weaknesses
  • A focused action plan for profitability and growth
  • Strategic direction to make confident, high-impact decisions
